===========================================
ABOUT CDC
===========================================

The Career Development Cell (CDC) at IIT Kharagpur is responsible for:
- Campus placements and recruitment drives
- Internship opportunities for students
- Industry-academia interaction
- Career counseling and guidance
- Coordinating company visits and pre-placement talks
- Managing placement statistics and records

Official Website: https://cdc.iitkgp.ac.in
